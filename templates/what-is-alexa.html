What is Alexa? What is Google Home?

It’s obvious, surely? They're voice-controlled assistants, built in to various different devices that sit in your home or travel around with you. That’s certainly one answer, but I’m interested in digging deeper.

Think of it this way: a car is a vehicle, but it’s also a technology that helped create the suburb, something that represents freedom and even sexual discovery for some people, and reminds others of mind-numbing daily commutes. A car's cultural meanings go far beyond being just a means of transport.

It's these cultural meanings I want to study, as part of my Masters in Digital Anthropology. And I could really do with your help.
